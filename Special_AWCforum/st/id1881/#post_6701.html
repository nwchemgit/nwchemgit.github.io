patch needed
Could you please apply the following patch to your NWChem installation?
http://www.nwchem-sw.org/images/Cosmo_meminit.patch.gz

Here is how to apply the patch


cd $NWCHEM_TOP
wget http://www.nwchem-sw.org/images/Cosmo_meminit.patch.gz
gzip -d Cosmo_meminit.patch.gz
patch -p0 < Cosmo_meminit.patch
